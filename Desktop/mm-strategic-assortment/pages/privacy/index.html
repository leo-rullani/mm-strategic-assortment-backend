2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the internal MM Strategic Assortment web application and its related backend services.

The application is intended exclusively for authorised business users. It supports internal assortment planning, alignment, execution, collaboration and documentation.

3. Personal Data We Process

Depending on the use and technical configuration of the application, the following categories of personal data may be processed:

  • Account information: name, business email address, user ID, assigned role and access permissions.
  • Authentication information: information and session data required to sign in and protect user accounts.
  • Application content: boards, tasks, assortment decisions, comments, attachments, assignments and status changes.
  • Activity information: change history, timestamps and actions associated with a user account.
  • Technical information: browser or device information, error records, login events and security-relevant system logs.
  • Support communications: information submitted when requesting assistance or reporting a problem.

Users must not enter sensitive personal data into the application unless this is expressly authorised and required for an approved business purpose.

4. Purposes of Processing

Personal data may be processed to:

  • Create, manage and secure user accounts.
  • Provide the functions of the MM Strategic Assortment application.
  • Support assortment planning, alignment, execution and documentation.
  • Assign responsibilities and maintain a traceable change history.
  • Enable collaboration, comments, notifications and internal communication.
  • Provide technical support and resolve errors.
  • Protect the application, company information and users against misuse or unauthorised access.
  • Meet applicable legal, regulatory and internal governance obligations.
  • Establish, exercise or defend legal claims where necessary.

5. Legal Framework

Personal data is processed in accordance with applicable Swiss data protection law, including the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection.

Processing takes place where it is necessary to operate and secure the application, perform employment-related or contractual responsibilities, comply with legal obligations or pursue legitimate business interests.

Where consent is legally required for a specific processing activity, consent will be requested separately.

Where European data protection law applies, processing may additionally be based on contract performance, legal obligations, legitimate interests or consent, depending on the relevant activity.

6. Access and Recipients

Personal data is accessible only to persons who require access for an authorised business purpose.

This may include:

  • Authorised employees and responsible managers.
  • Application and system administrators.
  • Internal support and governance functions.
  • Approved IT and service providers acting under appropriate confidentiality and data-processing obligations.

Personal data may also be disclosed if required by law, by a competent authority or for the establishment, exercise or defence of legal claims.

Personal data processed through this application is not sold.

7. International Data Transfers

If personal data is transferred to, stored in or made accessible from a country outside Switzerland, the transfer will take place only where permitted by applicable law.

Where required, appropriate safeguards may include an adequacy decision, recognised contractual clauses or another legally permitted transfer mechanism.

8. Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable legal, contractual and internal record-keeping obligations.

Retention periods may depend on the user-account lifecycle, business requirements, security requirements, statutory obligations and the need to resolve disputes or protect legal claims.

Data that is no longer required will be deleted, anonymised or otherwise handled in accordance with applicable requirements.

9. Browser Storage and Sessions

The application may use technically necessary browser storage, authentication tokens or session information to provide login, security and core application functionality.

These technologies are intended to operate and secure the application and are not intended for advertising or cross-site tracking.

10. Data Security

Appropriate technical and organisational measures are used to protect personal data against unauthorised access, accidental loss, misuse, alteration or disclosure.

Access is restricted according to business responsibilities and is granted only to authorised users.

No electronic system can guarantee absolute security. Users must therefore protect their credentials, follow internal security requirements and report suspected misuse or security incidents without delay.

11. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law and its limitations, individuals may have the right to:

  • Request information about the processing of their personal data.
  • Request access to their personal data.
  • Request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Request deletion or destruction where legally permitted.
  • Object to or request restriction of certain processing activities.
  • Request the release or transfer of certain personal data in a portable format where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for future processing where processing is based on consent.

Identity verification may be required before a request is processed. Certain rights may be restricted where processing or retention is required by law, necessary to protect overriding interests or required for legal claims.

Requests can be submitted using the contact information in Section 1.

Individuals may also contact the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner if they believe their data protection rights have been infringed.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the application, its data-processing activities or applicable legal requirements change.

The current version will be made available within the application.
