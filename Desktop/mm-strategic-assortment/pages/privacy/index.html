Privacy Policy

Effective date: 19 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your information when you use our application and its related backend services (the “Service”).

Application

Scope

This Policy applies to all use of the Service, including our web application and backend systems.

Information

Personal Data We Process

  • Account data: your full name, email address and a hashed password.
  • Content you create or upload: for example tasks, boards, comments, attachments and Strategic Assortment documents.
  • Communications: messages you send to us, such as support requests by email or phone.

We do not intend to collect special categories of personal data. Please refrain from sharing such information in the Service.

Use of data

Purposes of Processing

  • Provide, operate and maintain the Service;
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts;
  • Enable collaboration and notifications, such as comment notifications by email;
  • Store and manage boards, tasks and Strategic Assortment documents;
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support;
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Lawful processing

Legal Bases

  • Switzerland (FADP/DSG): processing in accordance with the principles of good faith, proportionality, purpose limitation and transparency for the purposes described above.
  • EEA/UK (GDPR, if applicable): performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)), compliance with legal obligations (Art. 6(1)(c)), our legitimate interests in providing a secure and reliable Service (Art. 6(1)(f)) and consent where required for optional features (Art. 6(1)(a)).

Third parties

Sharing of Personal Data

We do not sell your data. We share personal data only with:

  • Trusted service providers, such as hosting and email delivery providers, under confidentiality and data processing obligations;
  • Authorities or third parties when required by law or to protect legal rights;
  • Entities involved in a business transaction, such as a merger or reorganization, with appropriate safeguards.

Cross-border processing

International Data Transfers

If personal data is transferred outside Switzerland, the EEA or the UK, we rely on recognized safeguards such as adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses.

Storage period

Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described, to comply with legal obligations or to resolve disputes. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data unless retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as system backups.

Protection

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, loss or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data subjects

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction and portability of your personal data; object to processing based on legitimate interests; and withdraw consent at any time. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, such as the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) or your local data protection authority in the EEA or UK.

Age requirements

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Updates

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.
